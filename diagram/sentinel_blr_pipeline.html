3 layers
▼
Layer 5 · License Plate OCR
PaddleOCR PP-OCR-4 · Temporal 3-Frame Softmax Fusion · Levenshtein Outlier Filter
License Plate OCR — PP-OCR-4 runs on each frame independently; softmax scores across 3 consecutive frames are fused for higher accuracy. Levenshtein distance filters spurious single-character mutations.
PaddleOCR PP-OCR-4 Softmax Fusion Levenshtein
Layer 5E · Repeat Offender Tracking
Cross-Junction Plate Index · 24h/7d/30d Lookback · MV Act 2019 & 210A
Repeat Offender Tracking — Plate numbers are indexed across all junctions. Lookback windows at 24h, 7d, and 30d surface habitual offenders, which escalates enforcement under MV Act 2019 & section 210A.
Cross-Junction 24h/7d/30d MV Act 2019 210A
Layer 6 · Evidence Packet Generator
Annotated Frame + GradCAM · UTC Timestamp · GPS · SHA-256 Hash · BIS 0–100
Evidence Packet Generator — Produces a tamper-evident bundle: annotated frame with GradCAM saliency overlay, GPS-tagged location, UTC timestamp, SHA-256 integrity hash, and a BIS confidence score (0–100).
GradCAM UTC Timestamp GPS SHA-256 BIS Score

PostgreSQL + TimescaleDB · DuckDB Batch Analytics · Nightly 02:00 Aggregation

Urgency Score Recency Decay LoRA Weekly Tuning
